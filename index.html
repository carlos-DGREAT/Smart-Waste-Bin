The Smart Waste Bin.

Revolutionizing Waste Management in Baguio City

A Sustainable Solution for Public Place Waste

Bin-e is an innovative AI-powered smart waste bin, specifically designed for use in public areas to facilitate waste recycling. Its key features include an automatic opening cover, an AI-based recognition system, and automatic sorting, as well as fill level control, real-time data management, and an app for generating statistical reports.

  • A sustainable and cutting-edge alternative to conventional garbage disposal in public spaces is an intelligent e-bin. Smart e-bins, instead of conventional trash cans, are outfitted with cutting-edge technology that improves waste collection and management in terms of effectiveness, cost, and environmental friendliness.
  • Smart e-bins employ sensors to gauge the volume of waste inside. They may notify waste collection staff when the container is full, preventing unnecessary visits and increasing the efficiency of waste collection. In addition, they have separate sections for various waste types, which makes it simpler to filter and dispose of the waste that is gathered.

Spectroscopy and Image processing

The smart bin uses Fourier-transform infrared spectroscopy and image processing to measure the spectrum absorption rate of each element, enabling it to classify different types of trash based on their distinct energy.

Ultrasonic sensor

It is utilized inside the container of the smart bin, this monitors the fill level of the container which makes it easier to track which container is already full.

Proximity sensor

Used to detect the presence of an object or waste material in close proximity to the bin. This information is then used to trigger various actions such as opening the bin lid, activating the waste recognition system, or generating a fill-level alert.

How it Works

AUTOMATIC OPENING COVER

To make it easy for us and specially for people who have allergies or sensitive skin

AI-BASED RECOGNITION

By utilizing an advanced AI-powered recognition system, Bin-e is capable of accurately identifying the type of waste with a leading precision rate of over 92% globally, allowing users to effortlessly deposit waste into the bin.


AUTOMATIC SORTING

Once the waste has been identified, it will be automatically sorted and transported to the appropriate fraction without any manual intervention.

FILL LEVEL CONTROL

Bin-e is equipped with fill-level sensors that monitor the level of waste and determine when the bin needs to be emptied. You will receive a notification on your phone when the bins reach capacity.

REAL-TIME DATA MANAGEMENT

The data collected by Bin-e is transmitted to a cloud platform where it is processed in real-time.

APP FOR STATS REPORT

Our app provides you detailed statistics about the waste you collected, data for optimizing waste collection and the technical status your devices.
